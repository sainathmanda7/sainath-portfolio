Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection and Use

This website is a static digital portfolio. I do not actively collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) from visitors on backend databases.

2. Real-Time IP Geolocation (Interactive Feature)

For educational and interactive purposes, this website features a dynamic terminal sequence that temporarily retrieves your public IP address to display your approximate geographic location on the screen. This process utilizes a third-party geolocation service (ipapi.co). Strictly No Logging: This data is used solely for a real-time visual effect on your browser. I do not store, log, save, or track your IP address.

3. Third-Party APIs

This site fetches public open-source contribution data directly from GitHub. When this section loads, your browser makes a direct request to GitHub's servers.
